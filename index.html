Our Heritage

Established in Kaduna's industrial heartland, Kaduna Textile Industries stands as a testament to Nigeria's manufacturing resilience. Our state-of-the-art facility employs over 500 skilled workers, producing millions of meters of premium textiles annually.

We specialize in African print fabrics, shirting materials, uniform fabrics, and industrial textiles for the Nigerian market. With ISO-certified quality control and sustainable production practices, we're committed to reviving Nigeria's textile glory while supporting local cotton farmers and creating dignified employment.

Our fabrics tell Nigerian stories, worn with pride across West Africa.

Manufacturing Excellence

State-of-the-art production facility representing the pinnacle of modern textile manufacturing in Nigeria

Our production facility represents the pinnacle of modern textile manufacturing in Nigeria. Equipped with advanced weaving machines, digital printing technology, and automated quality control systems, we maintain consistent output without compromising craftsmanship.

Our integrated process covers everything from yarn preparation to fabric finishing, dyeing, and printing. We implement sustainable practices including water recycling, solar power integration, and eco-friendly dye processes.
